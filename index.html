The Suite

Two tools. One workflow.

Start with the diagnostic to assess your program's compliance posture. Move to the audit tool to operate against it with live data. Both are interactive, self-contained, and run entirely in your browser.

01 · Diagnostic

NAIA Track & Field Eligibility & Compliance Readiness Assessment

A 25-item diagnostic scoring your program across six weighted domains — eligibility certification, PTD monitoring, transcripts, recruiting, rules education, and audit readiness. Outputs a composite readiness percentage, risk tier, and prioritized 30-day and 31–90-day action plan.

About the Suite

Built on dissertation research.

The EPACC difference

EPACC is the only athletic compliance consulting firm built on empirical doctoral research connecting governance systems to academic performance metrics. Our work integrates the Burke-Litwin Organizational Change Model, the ADKAR behavioral change framework, and Compliance Theory to help athletic departments navigate complex compliance landscapes while protecting academic performance outcomes.

This suite reflects that approach. It is not a marketing checklist. It is the same diagnostic and operational logic used in institutional engagements, packaged for self-service use by athletic departments at the program level.

Coverage: NCAA Division I, II, III; NAIA; NJCAA. Tooling adapts compliance references and benchmarks based on division selection.
